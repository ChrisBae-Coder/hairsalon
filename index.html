PRE SERVICE

THINGS YOU SHOULD KNOW
BEFORE COLOURING YOUR HAIR

01
Don’t wash your hair for at least 72 hours after getting your colour done washing too soon will fade your colour more quickly.

02
Attending your appointment with oily or dirty hair will affect the way your hair lightens because of the oil barrier. Wash your hair 2 days prior to attending your colour appointment.

03
Don’t use any grey touch up mascara’s or sprays for 1-2 weeks prior to your colour. *Tip : these should also be salon quality buying these from supermarket can cause serious breakage.

04
Don’t use PURPLE shampoo for at least 2 weeks or more before your colour appointment.
